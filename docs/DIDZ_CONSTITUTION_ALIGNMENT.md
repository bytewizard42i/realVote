# DIDz Constitution Alignment

> **Pointer doc**: This repo conforms to the DIDz Constitution.
> The canonical copy lives at `DIDz-io/docs/DIDZ_CONSTITUTION.md`.

This repo (realVote) is a consumer of the DIDz engine and is bound by the
DIDz Constitution's root articles (I–VIII). Specifically relevant:

- **Article II §6** (human identity and proof of life), zVoting requires
  POL within ~1 year, plus eligibility credentials and a one-time election
  grant.
- **Article IV §14** (selective disclosure), voters prove eligibility
  without revealing vote choice.
- **Article VII §27** (violations), voting after death is a constitutional
  violation.
